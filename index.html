Welcome to Cyber-Quiz Challenge

Before you proceed, Please read our instructions in other to help you do better in your quiz Challenge.
  • Welcome to Cyber-Quiz Challenge:
    Before you begin, make sure you're in a distraction-free environment. Grab a pen and paper if you prefer to jot down notes. The quiz consists of multiple-choice questions, so choose the best option that fits the question. Let's get started!
  • Timing and Progress:
    You will have the opportunity to re-select an option if your initial choice is incorrect. Upon making a selection, the system will indicate whether the answer is right or wrong. If the answer is wrong, a message will be displayed, informing you of the correct answer in a clear paragraph format. Progress will be tracked and shown at the bottom of the page just after the last question option. Please double-check your responses before proceeding to the next question. We encourage you to take note of the questions you missed, as this system is designed to help you learn and improve more effectively.
  • Mandatory Attempt
    Please note that it is a MUST that you will attempt all questions before proceeding to the next question. This will further ensure that you are ready and eager to answer all of the questions.
  • Scoring and Results:
    Each correct answer will earn you points, but there is no negative marking for wrong answers. At the end of the quiz, you'll receive your score, and if you scored well, you can share it with your friends on social media!
  • Technical Issues:
    To ensure the best experience, use a modern web browser and a stable internet connection. Avoid refreshing or leaving the quiz page while attempting the quiz, as it may result in data loss.
